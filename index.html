Software Development Life Cycle (SDLC)

SDLC Phases

Phase Description Roles
Project Initiation This is the phase where we start the project. Product Owner
Requirement Definition All the information during this phase is gathered from customers to make a product even for what they excpect. Business Analyst
Implement or Coding This starts once the people who make it get the cut price. Software Developer
Testing Testing happens when the coding is finished and the studies are published for testing. Test Engineer
Deployment Once the thing that is being made is done is tested, it is released in the environment. Devops_Eng
Maintenance After the position of a product on the production habitat, supporting it can help the creators. Devops_Eng

SDLC Roles

SDLC Role Description
Project Manager Also known as the task manager of the whole project
Product Owner The person who does this part of the project is responsible for mostly the return on investment
Software Developer This part is a set of computer programs and connected documentation data
UI Designer The person who does this role works closely with the experience from a user
Technical Architech The person who does this role has the responsibility to design the structure of new systems and other technology
Business Analysis This role is used for the professional who closey works with stakeholders
Test Engineer The role of this is to check all of the materials fully, makers and mechanical or electrical systems to make sure that people get good quality products
Devops Engineer The person who does this role has to introduce different ways to stable needs throughout the whole cycle, proccesses and gadgets/tolls from coding to make to maintenance and future add-ons
